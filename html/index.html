ABOUT US

We want to have a safe video for everyone!

Bleepy is a Tagalog and English profanity blocker that will help the Gaming Video Creators also known as Game Streamers. This will help them to remove the profanities in their recorded videos playing a game with the use of machine learning and algorithm. Our mission is to support the local streamers here in the Philippines to have a better content in their channels and eliminate the profanities that restrict by community guidelines provided by the Social Media platforms in which game streamers upload their videos.
